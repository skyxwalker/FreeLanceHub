FreelanceHub

The Freelancer Job Portal, built using Laravel, is a dynamic platform designed to connect freelancers and customers seamlessly. It allows freelancers to create accounts, post jobs, and accept job requests, with all activities subject to admin approval to ensure quality and security. Customers can browse jobs, hire freelancers, and manage their payment histories. The admin oversees user accounts, job approvals, and payments while maintaining a feedback-driven ranking system that motivates freelancers to deliver high-quality work. The platform features a secure payment workflow, where payments are held by the admin until job completion, ensuring trust and transparency between parties. This project showcases a robust, user-friendly, and efficient solution for managing freelance services.
